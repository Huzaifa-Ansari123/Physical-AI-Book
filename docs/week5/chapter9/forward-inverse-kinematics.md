# Forward and Inverse Kinematics

Mathematical foundations for humanoid motion.

## Overview

Kinematics describes the motion of robot bodies without considering forces.

## Forward Kinematics

- **Definition**: Joint angles to end-effector pose
- **Method**: Chain of transformations
- **Applications**: Visualization, collision checking

## Inverse Kinematics

- **Definition**: End-effector pose to joint angles
- **Challenges**: Multiple solutions, singularities
- **Methods**: Analytical, numerical, learning-based
