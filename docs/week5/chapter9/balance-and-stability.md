# Balance and Stability

Keeping humanoid robots upright and stable.

## Overview

Balance control is fundamental to bipedal locomotion and manipulation.

## Stability Concepts

- **Center of Mass (CoM)**: Mass distribution
- **Center of Pressure (CoP)**: Ground contact
- **Zero Moment Point (ZMP)**: Dynamic balance
- **Support Polygon**: Stable region

## Balance Strategies

- Ankle strategy
- Hip strategy
- Stepping strategy
- Push recovery
