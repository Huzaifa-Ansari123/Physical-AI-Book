# Glossary

Key terms and definitions used throughout the course.

## A

- **Actuator**: A device that converts energy into motion
- **ASR**: Automatic Speech Recognition
- **Articulation**: A system of connected rigid bodies

## C

- **CoM**: Center of Mass
- **CoP**: Center of Pressure
- **CPG**: Central Pattern Generator

## D

- **DOF**: Degrees of Freedom
- **Dynamics**: Study of forces and motion

## F

- **Forward Kinematics**: Computing end-effector pose from joint angles

## G

- **Gait**: Pattern of locomotion

## I

- **IK**: Inverse Kinematics
- **IMU**: Inertial Measurement Unit

## K

- **Kinematics**: Study of motion without forces

## L

- **LiDAR**: Light Detection and Ranging

## N

- **NLU**: Natural Language Understanding

## R

- **ROS**: Robot Operating System
- **RL**: Reinforcement Learning

## S

- **SDF**: Simulation Description Format
- **Sim-to-Real**: Transferring from simulation to reality

## U

- **URDF**: Unified Robot Description Format

## Z

- **ZMP**: Zero Moment Point
